ProfileBlocks

A blockchain-powered platform for secure, verifiable, and student-centric credential management in Hong Kong and beyond.

Combat Credential Fraud

Protects against forgeries and scams by storing cryptographic proofs of every certificate on the Ethereum blockchain. Immutable, tamper-proof, and instantly auditable.

Hierarchical Institution Management

Supports complex education structures—university, faculty, department—mirroring Hong Kong’s real-world needs and allowing delegated issuance and control.

Student-Controlled Profiles

Students group certificates into shareable, cryptographically signed profiles for one-click verification—empowering ownership and privacy.

Instant, One-Click Verification

Employers or universities verify credentials instantly—no phone calls, emails, or paperwork. Verification is public, auditable, and privacy-preserving.

How ProfileBlocks Addresses Pain Points

  • Manual, Slow Verification? Replaced by instant, self-serve verification links and QR codes.
  • Forgery & Fake Certificates? All records cryptographically signed and stored on blockchain—impossible to forge or alter.
  • Complex Institutional Needs? Flexible support for hierarchical permissions and custom certificate types to match any organization.
  • Privacy Concerns? No personal data on-chain; all sensitive info secured off-chain and compliant with Hong Kong privacy laws.
  • Poor Usability? Modern, mobile-friendly design for students, institutions, and verifiers. No technical knowledge required.
